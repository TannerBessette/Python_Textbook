Filter down to keep recent years and NBA starters

I think a fair bar is a player must have started at least half of the games in an NBA season - so 42 games. Letâ€™s also only keep 21st century, since the game has evolved so much since before 2000. Also exclude the year 2025, because the season is active, and there has not been an all-star game.

# specify the seasons:
joined_all_star_data <- joined_all_star_data |>
  filter(season > 1999) |>
  filter(season != 2025)

# specify the number of starts each player must have (at least half of the season):
joined_all_star_data <- joined_all_star_data |>
  filter(gs > 41)
